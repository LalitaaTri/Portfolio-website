Featured Speakers

Dr. Rajesh Kumar

Cultural Historian & Author

Renowned expert in Tamil heritage with over 30 years of research in ancient traditions and cultural preservation. Author of 15 books on Tamil history.

Priya Bharathi

Master Bharatanatyam Dancer

International acclaimed classical dancer who has performed in over 40 countries. Founder of the Bharathi Dance Academy and cultural ambassador.

Prof. A. Subramaniam

Tamil Literature Scholar

Professor Emeritus specializing in ancient Tamil texts and Sangam literature. Winner of the Sahitya Akademi Award for Tamil literary contributions.

Arun Krishnan

Carnatic Music Virtuoso

Legendary veena player and vocalist who has preserved traditional Carnatic music for four decades. Mentored over 200 musicians worldwide.

Chef Meera Raman

Traditional Cuisine Expert

Master chef specializing in authentic Tamil cuisine. Author of "Flavors of Tamil Nadu" cookbook and UNESCO Cultural Heritage Ambassador.

Vikram Patel

Cultural Activist & Community Leader

Young leader dedicated to preserving Tamil culture among diaspora communities. Founder of Global Tamil Youth Network with presence in 25 countries.

Our Partners

Proud to collaborate with organizations that share our vision of cultural preservation

Tamil Heritage Foundation

Leading organization dedicated to preserving and promoting Tamil cultural heritage through educational programs and community initiatives.

Classical Arts Academy

Premier institution for traditional Indian classical arts, offering comprehensive training in dance, music, and theatrical arts to students of all ages.

Global Tamil Community

International network connecting Tamil communities worldwide, fostering cultural exchange and supporting diaspora families in preserving their roots.

Heritage Research Institute

Academic institution focused on research and documentation of South Asian cultural heritage, publishing scholarly works and organizing symposiums.
